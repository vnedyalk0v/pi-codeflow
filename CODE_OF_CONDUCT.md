# Code of Conduct

pi-codeflow follows a concise Contributor Covenant-style standard.

## Our pledge

We aim to make participation welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free for
everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while staying accountable for impact.
- Accept feedback gracefully.
- Focus criticism on ideas and work, not people.

## Unacceptable behavior

The following are not acceptable:

- harassment;
- discriminatory language;
- personal attacks;
- trolling;
- publishing private information.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, restrict participation, or take
other reasonable action to protect the community.
